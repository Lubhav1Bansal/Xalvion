Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?
For a standard 3-5 page website, it typically takes 1 to 2 weeks depending on how fast you can provide the content (text and images) and feedback.
Do I need to pay for hosting and domain separately?
Yes. My pricing covers the design and development. I will help you choose and set up a domain and hosting plan (like Hostinger or Vercel), but those are separate third-party costs you will own directly.
Why should I hire a beginner?
Because I have the time and motivation to give your project 100% of my focus. You get modern, fresh designs using the latest tools at a fraction of the cost of established agencies, as I am building my portfolio.
